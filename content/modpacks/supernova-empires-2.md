Loader: Forge

This is my new modpack it's based on ProjectE (like the others), but with some limitations, it's a bit difficult to craft items.

Now you can have a lot of security thanks to Securitycraft with provindes a reinforced set of blocks, You can troll your friends with Securitycraft mines and a lot's of other things.

We will guide you from start to finish based on quests, enjoy all the upgrades from vanilla items and your rewards.

We hope you enjoy the Draconic Evolution With aditional difficulty and Refined + Reborn Storage for your craftings.

We teach how to use roost and get your items.

This modpack have an extra quests (kill entities) for more fun, hope you enjoy your hunting time.
